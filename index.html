About me

Keijiro Nakagawa is a Senior Solutions Architect at Akamai Professional Services and a Visiting Research Fellow at University of Tokyo. He received PhD from University of Tokyo. He is interested in Sustainable Delay-Tolerant-Networking, Wildlife Enviroment Monitoring and Intergenerational Multi-hop Network among Animals.
